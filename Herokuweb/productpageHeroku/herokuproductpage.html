Heroku Products

Learn more about the suite of products that underpin today’s most innovative AI Platform as a Service.

Heroku

Heroku is a fully managed container-based cloud platform, with integrated data services and a powerful ecosystem, for deploying and running modern apps. The Heroku developer experience is based on an app-centric approach to software delivery, and integrates with today’s most popular developer tools and workflows.

More Products

Heroku Platform

The Heroku platform runs your apps inside dynos — smart containers which execute on a reliable, fully managed runtime environment. You can deploy code written in Node.js, Ruby, Java, PHP, Python, Go, Scala, or Clojure or use a custom buildpack to deploy apps in another language you choose. And you can scale your app instantly by increasing the number and type of dyno your app runs in.

More on the Heroku Platform

Heroku Platform

Heroku AI provides a streamlined platform for building, deploying, and scaling AI applications and agents. Access an opinionated set of models from the world’s top AI providers to enable faster development with Managed Inference and Agents, along with extensibility through Model Context Protocol (MCP). As an AI PaaS, Heroku gives developers the infrastructure, tools, and managed services to quickly bring AI applications and agents to market—all within the trusted Heroku developer experience, with our hallmark simplicity.
